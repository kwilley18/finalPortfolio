About

Web Development

I've followed the 3-step UX design process to create local business websites. I considered all the needs that the business had in order to develop a efficient website that displays their business in a creative and decorative way. For the layouts, I use figma. In order to display my designs, I've utilized JS frameworks such as React.js, AJAX, and Node.js

UI/Visual design

I'm a skilled visual designer with an eye for graphics, typography, layout, and color. I utilize resources such as Bootstrap to create responsive layouts. When it comes to graphics, I love utilizing Blender to create 3D graphics on the X,Y, Z coordinate system. I use my photography skills such light, line, shape, and texture to bring the design.

User Research

I believe it's important to gather feedback from users to decide what type of product they are looking for and how to use it. I make sure to consider all types of users such as ones with special needs. It makes designing a product more useful for everyone from different backgrounds.

Cognitive Science

I enjoy empathizing with users in order to understand the way they think. It makes the design process much easier because I am able to create a single product that represents the needs of everyone.
